About Koroks


Idk what these things are

These things are called Koroks, they appear in a magic cloud whenever you finish a minigame and are the givers of the Korok Seeds, each Korok gives a single Korok seed then vanishes.

Because of the disappearing nature of these Koroks, it quickly becomes almost impossible to keep track of what Korok Seeds the player has collected, providing an additional healthy dose of frustration when searching for Korok Seeds.
