Big Korok

This is the biggest Korok, its name is Hetsu


Give Seeds

When the player has collected enough Korok Seeds, they can give them to Hetsu


Maracas

Hetsu then does a song-and-dance routine with his maracas, with lots of confetti


Upgrade

When the dance is done, the player's inventory is very slightly upgraded


Hide and Seek

Hetsu moves locations as the player gives it more korok seeds, so good luck actually finding it
