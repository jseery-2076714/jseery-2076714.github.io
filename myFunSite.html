About Korok Seeds


They're almost useless

Korok Seeds are an item in the videogame The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild, which are collected by completing minigames throuhgout the game. There are exactly 900 Korok Seeds.

These Korok Seeds can be traded for upgraded inventory space, but there is no reason to upgrade more than a few times, making about 800 out of the 900 Korok Seeds basically worthless.
